Abnormality Pages

Floor of Art

Tier I Pages

Echoes From The Beyond Abnormality: Fragment of the Universe Awakening [All Characters]
Deal 5-10 Stagger damage to all enemies. All allies recover 5-10 Stagger Resist.

"You see a song in front of you. It's approaching, becoming more colorful by the second."


Tentacles Abnormality: Fragment of the Universe Awakening [Single Ally]
On a successful Pierce attack, reduce the max Stagger Resist of target by 5%. (Up to 50%)

"The moment its tentacles penetrate you, it'll drain all the interest for the life you're currently living out of you."


Pebble Abnormality: Child of the Galaxy Breakdown [Single Ally]
At the start of each Scene, three random pages in hand gain the 'Pebble' status.
Recover 3-7 HP upon using pages with the Pebble.
Lose 1-3 HP upon using pages without the Pebble.

"“Do you like it? More than you like me? Isn't it obvious? I know everything. Whatever you do, wherever you go.”"


Thorns Abnormality: Porccubus Awakening [Single Ally]
On a successful Pierce attack, inflict 1 Thrill to the target.
Against targets with 5 or more Thrill, deal 2-7 additional damage and Stagger damage and remove all stacks of Thrill.

"At first, all I had to do was be lightly pricked by one of the thorns. Now I have to rub my whole body against it to get the same high."


Pleasure Abnormality: Porccubus Breakdown [Single Ally]
Upon taking a hit, receive 2-5 bonus Stagger damage and restore 1 Light with a 50% chance. (Twice per Scene)

"Now it's time for my head to burst. Good day."


Laughing Powder Abnormality: Porccubus Breakdown [All Allies]
Upon taking a hit, recover 2-4 Stagger Resist if the character isn't staggered.

"When the powder that falls from the thorns becomes known to the world, people may forever sink into the swamp of intoxication."


Tier II Pages

Incomprehensible Abnormality: Fragment of the Universe Breakdown [Single Ally]
On a one-sided attack, inflict 1 Feeble and Disarm to the target at a 40% chance.
The likelihood increases by 10% for every successful attack. (Three times per engagement)

"Eventually, he succeeded in entering the domain of the ‘Fragment of the Universe’. However, it was obvious that his small brain couldn't withstand it."


Teardrop Abnormality: Child of the Galaxy Breakdown [Single Ally]
On death, all characters take 25% of this character's Max HP as damage. (Max. 30)

"I hear his whispers and breath. Pitch black darkness follows...just like the night I met him..."


A Token of Friendship Abnormality: Child of the Galaxy Awakening [All Allies]
For 3 Scenes, all allies recover 10% of Max HP at the start of the Scene. (Max. 12)

"The pebble dropped into your hand sparkles, sways, tickles, and eventually becomes the universe."


Flower Petals Abnormality: Alriune Breakdown [Single Ally]
Every time the librarian sustains damage greater than 10% of their Max HP, all allies recover 3-9 Stagger Resist.

"Shedding flower petal in place of tears, you spoke to me. “We were all part of earth once, so I plead you not to speak of the end here and now.”"


Autumn's Passing Abnormality: Alriune Awakening [Single Ally]
Upon a successful Pierce attack, inflict 1 Aroma.
(Aroma: At the start of the Scene, take Stagger Damage equal to the amount of Aroma and half the stack.)

"Bearing the hope to return to dust, it shall return to the grave with all that desires to live."


Ever-Repeating Performance Abnormality: The Silent Orchestra Awakening [Single Ally]
Add a 'Ever-repeating Performance' page to the hand: ‘Cost 3 - [On Use] Ends the Scene. At the start of the next Scene, user's HP and Stagger Resist return to the values they had when acquiring this page, and fully restore Light.’
The page is also included in the deck at the start of subsequent Acts.

"The performance does not end. And Da capo. And, Da capo al Fine"


Tier III Pages

Spring's Genesis Abnormality: Alriune Awakening [Single Ally]
Each Scene, a 'Petal' attaches to a random enemy. After the character with the Petal takes 4 hits in a single Scene, all characters take 3-7 Stagger damage, and the selected librarian becomes untargetable for the next Scene.

"Unwithering flowers violently blossom."


Fervent Adoration Abnormality: The Silent Orchestra Breakdown [Single Ally]
All enemies target random characters including their own allies for 2 Scenes. Enemies deal 2-4 additional damage with attacks while this effect lasts.

"The orchestra gives impetus to the music, bringing the entire department to its demise. When all the performers have gathered, the music that no one can hear but everyone can listen to begins."


The Finale Abnormality: The Silent Orchestra Awakening [Single Ally]
Whenever the selected librarian Staggers an enemy, all enemies lose 1-6 Light at the start of the next Scene.

"“The music shall perforate your entire being.”"
